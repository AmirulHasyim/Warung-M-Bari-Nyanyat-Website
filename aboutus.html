    Little bit about Warung M'Bari Nyanyat

    Established in December 2021, the stall began its journey at Muara Pantai Kenangan, Bongawan, offering only two dishes: Char Kuey Teow and Laksa.

    The name of the stall, inspired by the Brunei slang “Membari Nyanyat,” meaning “to give a feeling of addiction,” reflects the spontaneous response of customers who fell in love with its food.

    Led by Ali Bin Patra, a retired military personnel, the stall relocated in May 2022 to a new backyard dining concept at Kg Silat, Jalan Pantai Kenangan, Bongawan.

    Over time, the business flourished, expanding its menu to more than 20 food items.

    Now registered under the “Pantai Kenangan Bongawan Business Partner Community,” the stall operates alongside more than five business partners, united under one brand and continuing to grow in popularity.

    Objectives of Warung M'Bari Nyanyat

    Objectives :
    >To provide delicious and high-quality food by paying attention to every detail, from the selection of fresh ingredients to the way of preparation, presentation and also satisfying dining experience, suitable for various tastes and preferences.
    > To ensure every customer feels valued and has a pleasant dining experience. Our goal is to create relationships with customers and be the place they always talk about.
    > To make Warung M'Bari Nyanyat the main food destination of choice, not only for residents in the immediate area but also residents from outside the area who are on vacation with family or friends.
